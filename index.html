Our Mission

Bayside Highlights finds its roots in the successes of our community. We are inspired by their interests and achievements in and out of the classroom. It is our hope that this publication becomes a platform to continue to showcase the diverse accomplishments of our students. We are especially grateful to Principal Martinez, AP Tessenholtz, and AP Hirata who are eager to help bring this vision to life.
